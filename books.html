Key ideas from some books I read

Books are like seeds. They can lie dormant for centuries and then flower in the most unpromising soil.

Sapiens

Yuval Noah Harari

"You could never convince a monkey to give you a banana by promising him limitless bananas after death in monkey heaven."

But you can do this to humans.

Euclid's Window

Leonard Mlodinow

This is the story of geometry from parallel lines to hyperspace, from Euclid Witten and from simple lines to string theory.

Written by Star Trek scriptwriter.

The Kite Runner

Khaled Hosseini

And that's the thing about people who mean everything they say. They think everyone else does too. The eyes are window to the soul.

It makes one cry a lot.

Hyperspace

Michio Kaku

In other words, the reason why the string theory cannot be solved is that twenty-first mathematics has not yet been discovered.

It transforms our vision.

Factfullness

Hans Rosling

Forming your worldview by relying on the media would be like forming your view about me by looking only at a picture of my foot.

This books brings optimism.

Cosmos

Carl Sagan

If a human disagrees with you, let him live. In a hundred billion galaxies, you will not find another. There is only one earth known so far.

We are a speck of dust.
